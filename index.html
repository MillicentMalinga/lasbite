LasBite

Reducing food waste one shared bite at a time.

Our solutions ensure food security for all urban communities, conscious food waste management and a better planet.

LasBite is a data-driven food-sharing network where neighbours connect with each other and with restaurants to share surplus food that would have otherwise been wasted.

Every year a third of the food produced is wasted. In Rwanda, Food waste accounts for 80% of the waste in landfills, yet 20% of the population is experiencing food insecurity.

The LasBite platform enables food service providers to sell excess food at discounted prices at the end of every business day. They get data metrics about their excess food production for responsible production. Households can share food they don't need. Those who need it can make arrangements to collect it.

LasBite is the power of data in accelerating responsible food consumption and waste management.
